Chantry Island tour schedule

We offer tours throughout the summer season from late May to Mid September. During June and July we offer the tour daily, check above for the exact schedule, it does change on a yearly basis.

Since the Chantry Island is also a federal bird sanctuary we are only able to bring 12 persons per tour. Therefore, call ahead to ensure your place on the boat.

Rates

$30 per person(includes HST), same price for children and adults.
